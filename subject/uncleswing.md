# 萌叔走路(Tilt Tilt)

这是一款非常困难的平衡类游戏，让萌叔走的更远吧！

This is a difficult game! Keep balance to walk longer.

点击屏幕开始，左右滑动控制平衡，当心香蕉皮！

Tap screen to start. Slide left/right to keep balance. Look out the bananas.
